Message from the principal

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to INDUSTRIAL TRAINING INSTITUTE Srinagar ( ITI Srinagar) which is in the service of nation, imparting craftsman training to the youth since 1957. Industrial training institute Srinagar is running under the Administrative control Of Directorate of Skill Development Department Jammu & Kashmir, implementing Craftsmen Training scheme sponsored by Director General Employment & Training (DGE&T) New Delhi Crafts man scheme was introduced by government of India in the year 1950 to ensure a steady flow of skilled workers in different trades for domestic industry to raise quantitatively & qualitatively the industrial production by systematic training, to reduce unemployment among youth by providing them employable training, to cultivate & nurture a technical /industrial attitude in the minds of the younger generation. The scheme was transferred to the state of Jammu & Kashmir in the year 1958 & ITI Srinagar was established in the year 1962. District Srinagar is located in south of Kashmir which is summer capital of J&K UT. ITI Srinagar is located at about 2.5 Kms from main town of Srinagar towards north.
